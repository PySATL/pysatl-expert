    PipelineComponents(distributions, criterion_selector, strategy, feature_extractor) ¶

    A registry container aggregating modular components for the expert system.

    Centralizes candidate distributions, selection logic, decision strategies, and profiling tools. This decoupling allows the pipeline logic to remain independent of specific mathematical or algorithmic implementations.

    AbstractStrategy ¶

    Bases: ABC

    Interface for the decision-making module of the expert system.

    The Strategy interprets statistical evidence (scores and sample stats) aggregated in a FeatureVector to select the most appropriate distribution. Allows for pluggable logic: from simple heuristics to ML-classifiers.
